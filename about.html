When I am not in class, I am sometimes doing the following things.

Working Out

Before I started this class I used to go to the gym in the morning. I like to start my day by working out, and I like all the older folks who are at the gym at 7am.

Because class is at 9:30, I don't feel like I have enough time to go to the gym before class. I don't like being rushed before classes and I don't want to be too tired.

Now I go to the gym after class. I don't like it as much because I don't know the people in the afternoons, and honestly they aren't as nice as the older people.

Playing Tennis

I have been playing sports competitively since I was a child. I started with soccer, which I will always love, and then picked up tennis. In between I did gymnastics and basketball.

I picked up tennis about 10 years ago, which isn't that long in the grand scheme of tennis playing. I played throughout highschool and in college for my school. College athletics are hard and I almost quit a few times, but I ened up sticking through it.

Now, I like to play with my friends casually. Most of my friends are not that good, so it is really easy and relaxed to play, although I do miss the intensity sometimes. I have also picked up pickleball, which is fun but not as much of a workout as tennis.

Watching Movies and Shows

I am no film bro, but I definitely enjoy watching movies and tv shows.

Some of my favorite movies include Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind, Bend it Like Beckham, Past Lives, and Surf's Up(you can't deny that it's a masterpiece). While I mostly enjoy light hearted movies, I do enjoy a good drama.

I have only recently got into watching movies, but I have always been a big fan of tv shows. Some of my favorites are The Bear, Derry Girls,Fleabag, and New Girl.

When I'm not doing any of these things, I enjoy spending time with my friends and family.
